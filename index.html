About

I am a software engineer at GE Capital in New Orleans.

I fell in love with software while earning my degree in electrical engineering from The Cooper Union. It started with C and Python, but I focus on web development in Javascript and Java these days.

I've been a volunteer with Operation Spark since 2014 teaching Javascript in their web development bootcamps for local opportunity youth.

I occasionally blog over at Silicon Spatula with my old friend @davidleshaw about tech and food.

I enjoy practicing my public speaking skills regularly. I've been a member of Toastmasters International since 2013, cofounding a club with @donnykanner and acting as an officer in my current club at GE.
